Welcome

Thank you for being here!

• Who am I?

     Greetings, chess enthusiast! If you have a passion for chess and love playing with friends, then you've come to the right place!
I am a professional chess player who shares your passion for the game and possesses extensive knowledge in web technology. After three years of experience, I proudly present to you TwiChess, a platform where chess meets innovation. I encourage you to explore my work and share your valuable feedback with me here. Your thoughts mean the world to me.

• Why play here?

     While my platform may not be the most optimized chess project online, I am committed to achieving perfection. I actively refine my platform to cater to your needs, constantly seeking ways to improve. I value your input and believe that together, we can grow and enhance the chess experience. TwiChess offers a diverse array of tools and resources to help you improve your chess skills in your own unique way. Once again, thank you for being a part of my community!

• About this project

     This project was made for a thrilling competition where I set out to improve my web development skills. It has been an exciting journey filled with valuable lessons. Throughout the development process, I entered deep into the intricacies of JavaScript, mastering its core mechanics and exploring its potential for creating dynamic and interactive web experiences. Additionally, I dived into CSS animation, discovering the power of visually engaging transitions and effects. I learned how to "breathe life" into a website by incorporating CSS animations to HTML elements. This project not only showcases my dedication to web development but also reflects my passion for creating immersive and captivating online spaces. I am thrilled to share this project with you.
