• Who are we?

     Hello chessmates! If you like chess, and want to play with your friends, then you're in the right place!
We are a team of profesional chess players, who are also passionate about web technology, and have decided to put our knowlege to the test. This is the result of work and knowlege gathered in over 3 years of experiene. If you'd like to review our work, make sure to check out the Contact Support page. We'd love to hear your thoughts.

• Why chose us?

     This may not be the most optimised chess project online, however, we strive for perfection and actively attempt to chisel our project to reach your needs. We are open to suggestions and hope that with you, we can grow and improve together. We offer an arsenal of tools at your dispozal, wich you can use to improve at chess, your own way. Again, thank you for being with us!
